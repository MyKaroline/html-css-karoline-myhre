Shipping & Delivery

We want your new jacket to reach you before the next downpour. All orders are packed and shipped from our warehouse in Oslo within 1–2 business days.

Delivery Options

Standard delivery (3–5 business days) is free on all orders over 999 kr. For orders below that, a flat rate of 59 kr applies.

Express delivery (1–2 business days) is available for 149 kr, so you're covered even if the forecast isn't on your side.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can follow your package every step of the way — from our door to yours.

International Shipping

We currently ship to all Nordic countries and most of Europe. International delivery takes 5–10 business days, with rates calculated at checkout. Customs fees may apply outside the EU/EEA.

Returns

Changed your mind? No problem. You have 30 days to return unworn items for a full refund. See our Returns page for details.

Questions?

Our support team is happy to help at support@rainydays.no — rain or shine.
