Returns & Refunds

Not the right fit? No worries. We want you to love your Rainy Days jacket, and if you don't, returning it is simple.

Our Return Policy

You have 30 days from the day you receive your order to return it. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached.

How to Return

Email us at support@rainydays.no with your order number, and we'll send you a prepaid return label. Pack the jacket in its original packaging if possible, attach the label, and drop it off at your nearest post office.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, your refund will be issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days. We'll send you an email confirmation as soon as it's processed.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? The fastest way is to return your item for a refund and place a new order. That way your new jacket is on its way before the clouds roll in.

Faulty Items

If your jacket arrives damaged or develops a fault, contact us right away at support@rainydays.no. We'll cover all costs and make it right with a replacement or full refund.
