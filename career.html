Careers at Rainy Days

We're a small team in Oslo on a mission to make the best rainwear in the Nordics. If you love good design, great products, and don't mind a bit of weather, you might fit right in.

Working With Us

At Rainy Days you won't be a small cog in a big machine. We're a tight-knit team where everyone's ideas count, whether you work with design, logistics, or customer support. Flat structure, flexible hours, and yes — a generous jacket discount.

Open Positions

We don't have any open positions right now, but we're always interested in meeting talented people. New roles are posted here as they open, so check back soon.

Open Application

Think you'd be a great fit? Send your CV and a few words about yourself to jobs@rainydays.no. We read every application and reply to all of them — rain or shine.

Internships

We occasionally take on interns within e-commerce, marketing, and design. If you're a student looking for real experience in a growing webshop, reach out at jobs@rainydays.no.
