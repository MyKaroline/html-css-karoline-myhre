About Rainy Days

Rainy Days was born in Oslo — a city with over 160 days of rain a year. We figured if the weather won't change, the jackets should.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, we started with a simple idea: rainwear shouldn't be a compromise between staying dry and looking good. What began as a small webshop with three jacket styles has grown into a collection trusted by thousands across the Nordics.

What We Believe

A jacket should last more than a season. That's why we focus on durable materials, taped seams, and timeless designs you'll want to wear year after year — not throw away when trends shift.

Made for the Outdoors

Whether you're hiking a fjell, biking to work, or just waiting for the bus in a downpour, our jackets are built to handle real Nordic weather. Tested in Norway, ready for anywhere.

Get in Touch

Want to know more? Say hello at hello@rainydays.no or visit our Contact page. We're always happy to talk jackets.
